Jaipur

Jaipur, the vibrant capital of Rajasthan, is famously known as the “Pink City” for its rose-hued architecture that paints the old city in charm and tradition.

Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, it’s one of India’s earliest planned cities, blending Vastu Shastra with urban design.

From majestic forts like Amer and palaces like Hawa Mahal to bustling bazaars and rich crafts, Jaipur is a living canvas of royal heritage and cultural splendor.
