Ujjain

Ujjain, one of the seven sacred cities of Hinduism, rests gracefully on the banks of the Shipra River in Madhya Pradesh.

It’s home to the revered Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga temple and hosts the grand Simhastha Kumbh Mela every 12 years, drawing millions of pilgrims.

Steeped in ancient wisdom, Ujjain was once a center of astronomy and learning, echoing the legacy of scholars like Varāhamihira and Bhaskaracharya.
